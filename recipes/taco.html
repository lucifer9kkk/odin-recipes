Vaggie Taco

Home

A vibrant and wholesome vegetarian taco features a hearty base of seasoned black beans and sautéed vegetables, offering a satisfying blend of textures and smoky flavors. These are served in warm, charred tortillas and topped with cool, creamy elements like avocado or crema, providing a refreshing contrast to the spicy, savory filling. It is a highly customizable dish that celebrates fresh produce and bold Mexican-inspired spices in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Corn or flour tortillas (small street-taco size)
  • Black beans (canned, rinsed and drained)
  • Bell peppers and onions (thinly sliced)
  • Corn kernels (fresh, frozen, or canned)
  • Olive oil or vegetable oil
  • Chili powder, cumin, and smoked paprika
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Avocado or guacamole
  • Fresh cilantro (chopped)
  • Cotija cheese or shredded Monterey Jack
  • Sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • Lime wedges (for serving)

Steps

  1. Sauté the sliced onions and bell peppers in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic, corn, and black beans to the pan once the vegetables soften.
  3. Season the mixture with chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally until the flavors are well combined.
  5. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or directly over a gas flame until slightly charred.
  6. Spoon a generous portion of the veggie and bean mixture into the center of each tortilla.
  7. Top the tacos with sliced avocado, crumbled cheese, and a dollop of sour cream.
  8. Garnish with a sprinkle of fresh cilantro and a squeeze of fresh lime juice.
  9. Serve immediately while the filling is hot and the tortillas are soft.
